Official Voting Guidelines

Terms of Service & Regulations
1

Voting Eligibility & Timeframe

  • Designated Voting Window: Voting is strictly permitted only during the officially scheduled timeframe.
  • Strict Deadlines: The system will automatically reject any votes attempted before the official start time or after the closing deadline.
  • Attendee Exclusivity: Voting is open only to verified participants and attendees of the event.
2

Voting Limits & Categories

  • Category Restriction (Boys): Each voter is entitled to cast exactly one (1) vote for a candidate in the Boys' category.
  • Category Restriction (Girls): Each voter is entitled to cast exactly one (1) vote for a candidate in the Girls' category.
  • Maximum Vote Limit: A single user can cast a maximum of two votes in total across the entire platform.
  • Irreversible Submissions: Once a vote is successfully submitted, it is final. Votes cannot be changed, edited, or canceled under any circumstances.
3

Location & Geofencing (Premises Rule)

  • Physical Presence Required: Voting is strictly geofenced. You must be physically located within the designated event premises to access the voting portal.
  • Location Services: Voters must grant location access on their devices to verify their presence inside the event boundaries.
  • No Location Spoofing: The use of VPNs, proxies, or fake GPS applications to bypass the location restriction is strictly prohibited and will result in an instant ban.
4

Account & Email Verification

  • Valid Email Requirement: All voters must register using a valid, active, and personal email address.
  • No Fake Accounts: The use of disposable, temporary, or bot-generated email accounts is strictly forbidden.
  • Mandatory Verification: Votes will only be counted after the user verifies their identity (e.g., clicking a confirmation link sent to their email or entering an OTP).
  • Single Identity: Voting on behalf of someone else or using another person’s email address/device is not allowed.
5

Anti-Fraud & Security Measures

  • Zero Tolerance for Bots: The use of automated scripts, bots, or third-party software to generate votes will trigger automatic deletion of those votes.
  • IP & Device Monitoring: The system will actively monitor IP addresses and device IDs. Suspicious mass voting from a single device or network will be flagged and voided.
  • System Integrity: Any malicious attempts to hack, manipulate, or overload the voting servers will result in an immediate ban and potential disciplinary action.
  • Audit Rights: The organizing committee reserves the right to audit the voting logs to identify and remove fraudulent activity before declaring the winners.
6

Code of Conduct & Fair Play

  • Candidate Disqualification: If a candidate or their campaign is found guilty of utilizing fake emails, bots, or location spoofing, the candidate will face immediate disqualification.
  • No Bribery: Offering money, gifts, or any other incentives in exchange for votes is strictly a violation of fair play rules.
  • No Coercion: Forcing, intimidating, or harassing other attendees to vote for a specific candidate is strictly prohibited.
7

Privacy, Technicalities & Final Authority

  • Data Privacy: Your email address and location data are strictly confidential. They are collected solely for vote authentication and will never be shared with third parties.
  • Technical Disclaimers: The organizers are not responsible for votes that fail to submit due to personal device malfunctions, poor cellular data, or local internet connectivity issues.
  • Result Confidentiality: Live voting tallies will remain hidden from the public to maintain fairness and excitement.
  • Final Authority: The organizing committee holds the ultimate authority over the voting process. Their decisions regarding disputes, rule violations, and final results are binding and final.

By participating in the voting process, you agree to abide by all the rules and regulations stated above.
