Voting Guidelines

Official Terms & Regulations for INCEPTRA 25

Eligibility & Participation

  • Voters must be physically present at the INCEPTRA 25 venue to cast a vote.
  • Only individuals with a valid event ticket or registration are eligible to participate.
  • Participants must be at least 18 years old or have guardian consent if required by local laws.
  • Staff members directly involved in managing the voting system are prohibited from voting.

Voting Schedule

  • Voting opens strictly at the designated start time announced by the organizers.
  • Voting closes precisely at the deadline; late submissions will be automatically rejected.
  • The system clock is synchronized with official global time; disputes regarding timing will not be entertained.
  • Any extensions or changes to the schedule will be announced via official PA systems.

Location Verification

  • GPS Required: Location services must be enabled on your device to access the voting portal.
  • Geofencing: Votes originating from outside the predefined GPS coordinates of the venue will be blocked.
  • No Spoofing: Use of VPNs, fake GPS apps, or proxies to bypass location checks results in an instant ban.
  • If location fails, please approach the technical help desk for manual verification.

Account & Verification

  • A valid email address or phone number is required for one-time password (OTP) verification.
  • Each unique identifier (email/phone) is linked to exactly one voting profile.
  • Disposable or temporary email addresses are strictly forbidden.
  • Keep your OTP confidential; do not share it with anyone, including volunteers.
  • Account sharing is prohibited; actions taken under your account are your responsibility.

Voting Mechanics

  • Maximum Votes: Each voter can cast exactly one (1) vote per category.
  • Categories: There are separate categories for Boys and Girls.
  • Finality: Once a vote is submitted, it cannot be changed, edited, or revoked.
  • Ensure you review your selection carefully before hitting the "Submit" button.

Anti-Fraud & Integrity

  • Zero Bots: Automated scripts or botting tools are illegal.
  • The system monitors IP addresses and device fingerprints for duplicate voting attempts.
  • Suspicious activity patterns (e.g., rapid-fire votes) will trigger an automatic flag.
  • Attempts to manipulate the server database will lead to legal action and event bans.
  • All votes are logged with timestamps and device info for audit trails.

Code of Conduct

  • Voters must respect the privacy of others; photography of other people's screens is banned.
  • No Bribery: Offering money or gifts for votes is a violation.
  • No Coercion: Forcing someone to vote for a specific candidate is prohibited.
  • Abusive language or harassment of the voting committee will result in removal.

Technical Requirements

  • Use a modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge) updated to the latest version.
  • Ensure stable internet connection; organizers are not liable for user connectivity issues.
  • Clear browser cache if the page fails to load properly.
  • Do not refresh the page while the vote is being processed.

Disputes & Results

  • Live vote counts are hidden to maintain fairness.
  • The organizing committee reserves the right to disqualify fraudulent votes.
  • In the event of a tie, a tie-breaking mechanism (e.g., coin toss or revote) will be announced.
  • Results declared by the committee are final and binding.

Data Privacy

  • Personal data collected is used solely for vote authentication.
  • Data is encrypted and stored securely; it will not be sold to third parties.
  • Voter anonymity is preserved in the final public results.
  • Data will be purged 30 days after the event conclusion.

Accessibility

  • Assistive technology users should ensure compatibility with the web portal.
  • Volunteers are available to assist visually or physically impaired voters.
  • Priority queues will be provided for those needing special assistance.

Candidate Conduct

  • Candidates are not allowed to campaign within 10 meters of the voting station.
  • Candidates found buying votes will be disqualified immediately.
  • Agents appointed by candidates must carry valid ID badges.
  • Candidates cannot touch or handle voter devices.

Device Regulations

  • One vote per device is preferred. If using a shared device, ensure proper logout.
  • Do not leave your device unattended while logged into the voting portal.
  • Jailbroken or rooted devices may face security restrictions.

Social Media & Publicity

  • Voters are encouraged to share participation but not reveal specific vote choices publicly if asked.
  • Recording video of the voting process (filling the form) is prohibited to ensure secrecy.
  • Hashtag usage for event promotion is welcomed, provided it does not harass others.

Final Authority

  • The organizing committee has the final say on any ambiguities in these rules.
  • Rules may be updated without prior notice; the latest version on the site applies.
  • By voting, you acknowledge that you have read and understood these 30 guidelines.

By participating, you agree to abide by the above regulations.
